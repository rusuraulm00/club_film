Home Rules Members Founders
  1. Be respectful to everyone
    • Listen to others' opinions without interrupting
    • No insults, name-calling, or rude comments
    • Keep the atmosphere friendly and open
  2. Arrive ready for the screening
    • Come before the film starts whenever possible
    • Bring only quiet snacks and drinks
    • Stay seated and keep noise low during the movie
  3. Join the discussion politely
    • Share thoughts after the film instead of during it
    • Respect different tastes and keep criticism constructive
    • Help the club stay welcoming for everyone
